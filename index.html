Getting Started

How to use these guides

Every guide is built the same way, so once you know one you know them all. Open a guide on the classroom laptop and work through it beside the machine.

💡
Jump in with the process map Each guide opens with a clickable process map and flowchart. Click any step to jump straight to it, or follow the guide top to bottom the first time through.
ℹ️
Expand each step for detail Steps stay collapsed so the guide is easy to skim. Click a step header to reveal its full sub-steps, callouts, and diagrams — only one step opens at a time to keep you focused.
✅
Check off as you go Each guide ends with an interactive checklist and a progress bar. Tick items as you complete them — the job is ready when everything is checked.
⚠️
Stuck on something? Every guide has a troubleshooting table near the bottom that pairs common problems with their likely cause and a fix. Check there before stopping a job.
